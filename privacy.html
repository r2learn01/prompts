Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Welcome to R2 AI Prompts. We believe in a frictionless and secure experience. Our platform is designed to be used without the need for registration, and we do not collect personal information. This policy outlines our simple approach to privacy and the acceptable use of our AI tools.

1. No Personal Data Collection

You can use R2 AI Prompts completely anonymously. There is no account creation process, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or any identifying information. We only temporarily process the prompts you enter to generate the requested content, and these are not linked to any personal identity.

2. Technical & Usage Data

To ensure the website runs smoothly, we may monitor general, non-identifiable technical metrics (such as site traffic, error rates, or device types). This helps our engineering team maintain the platform's stability and improve the quality of AI outputs, without tracking individual users.

3. User Preferences (Cookies/Local Storage)

We use minimal local storage strictly to remember your interface preferences, such as your choice of language (Arabic/English) and theme (Light/Dark mode). We do not use tracking cookies or share any browsing data with advertising networks.

4. Acceptable Use and Media Guidelines

We provide this AI tool to inspire creativity. However, users are strictly prohibited from using the platform to generate, manipulate, or distribute media for inappropriate, harmful, illegal, or deceptive purposes. This includes, but is not limited to, creating deepfakes, offensive material, hateful content, or violating intellectual property rights. Users bear full and sole responsibility for the content they generate and how they choose to use or publish it.

5. Policy Updates

As our platform and AI technologies continue to evolve, we may update this policy occasionally to reflect new features or usage guidelines. Any changes will be published directly on this page.
